Manage cycle-based repeating events in Google Calendar.

Easily make events repeat according to your school or organizations cycle-based calendar patterns with Cyclone, a Workspace Add-on for Google Calendar.

Get More Information Now

Operating on a 6, 7, or 10 day cycle offers schools many benefits. Calendar managment isn’t one of them.

Google Calendar only makes it easy to schedule events that repeat on a Monday - Friday schedule. Working with a different rotation of events makes it tough to schedule.

Increased Overhead

Scheduling repeating events individually wastes a lot of time that could be better spent on other things

Managing Multiple Calendars

Administrative assistants can have a lot of trouble scheduling meetings for multiple people

Class Analytics

It’s hard to figure out how many class meetings you have, or the total time you have with each class in a marking period.

Coordinating Classes

Aligning parallel sections of a class is a complex task when you can’t easily figure out when each class section is going to meet.

Cyclone Features

Cyclone is Workspace Add-on for Google Calendar makes it easy to schedule and analyze events, even events that repeat outside of a Monday - Friday pattern.

Fast, Easy, and Accurate Scheduling

Add and Delete recurring events with 1-click on any cycle/schedule pattern.

  • Cyclone Lives in Google Calendar. A simple interface is accessible from the Google Calendar sidebar. No .ics files, no uploading, no emails, no scheduling dozens of individual events.
  • One-click option for adding and deleting recurring events. Once users click an event, a single button creates repeating events through the end of the school year, or some other specified timeframe. Deleting connected recurring events is just as easy.
  • Schedule recurring events for other people Administrative assistants can add recurring cycle-based events for other people by easily selecting a shared calendar.
  • Daylight Savings and Time-Zone Aware. Events are scheduled with an awareness of time-zone specific patterns, including daylight savings.
  • School-day only scheduling. Recurring events can repeat every cycle, or every other cycle. Also supports scheduing recurring school day events on a weekly schedule, but only on school days so that recurring events won't be created during breaks or on other non-school days.
  • Optimized for speed. User actions are performed quickly because Calendar operations are optimized on the backend to minimize calls to Google's services.

Convenient Planning

View nuanced meeting statistics and genrate printable, customizable planners to help everyone understand their schedule.

  • Helpful analytics. Precalculated statistics like total meeting time, total number of meetings, and sequential event numbering.
  • Customizable planner options. Planners are generated using Google Sheets, so customizing event names and print formats is easy.
  • Easily setup unique colors in one Google Calendar. Specify a unique color for recurring each meeting group to help you quickly understand the nature of the meeting.

Easy Configuration.

Setting up Cylcone for your Google Worspace is painless and hassle-free.

  • Easy cycle configuration. Simply select an existing cycle calendar to provide a pattern for recurring day labels. 6-day, 7-day, 10-day cycles? No problem.
  • Workspace ready. Google Workspace Admins can quickly preconfigure a cycle calendar for all workspace users.
  • Accommodates your unique needs. Let us know what would help make Cyclone work better for your organization!
